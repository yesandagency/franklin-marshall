The center opened in the fall of 2018 and serves as the home for the Fire Science and Law Enforcement academies at Collin College. The center also provides expanded training and professional development opportunities for local fire departments and law enforcement agencies. Situated on 25 acres near the corner of Bloomdale Road and Redbud Boulevard in McKinney, Texas, the center consists of fire science training grounds, a reality-based simulation village for police training, three separate shooting ranges, nine classrooms and 200 parking spaces.
